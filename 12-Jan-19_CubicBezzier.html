Cubic Bezzier Function

I've stumbled upon the Cubic Bezzier Function for CSS. And i've figured out I have no Idea what it is. I've stumbled upon it on the Free Code Camp Curriculum "Applied Visual Design: Learn How Bezier Curves Work" . Unfortunately I don't understand particularly quite well what it does just yet, but from my understanding, the function's parameters requires 4 points (p0, p1, p2, p3). The starting point (p0) is always at x=0, and y=0, along a "1 x 1" grid. Likewise with the last point (p3), it is always located at x=1, and y=1. The remaining points are p1, and p2.
